About Me

hello there !


I am Arif Emre ARIKAN. I have had a chance to work with many different models of Siemens plcs such as many different s7-1200 series s7-1500, fixing malfunction issues on s7-300s and Siemens HMIs. Rather than Siemens I also worked with Fatek PLCs and Delta PLC and HMIs, Beijer HMI and SCADA systems.

The company I used to work with serves around the globe and we had a limited team. That required us working overtime. That’s why even though I had one and a half year of experience I had many opportunities to improve myself. I fixed malfunction issues on live working factories, built machines from scratch. I also have a good understanding for safety, measuring sensors especially on SICK branded ones.

Rather than PLC programming I have worked with automation projects without plcs. Also assembled control panels by myself many times.

I used to travel all around Turkey for machine installation and fixing.


I am currently working as a website administrator at esarpipekyolu.com. During the time I am trying to learn Web Development and I try to improve myself on this matter.
